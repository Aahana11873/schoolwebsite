Laxman Public School is a private school located in New Delhi, India. It was founded by Late Shri Laxman S Aggarwal. A. The school enrolls children from kindergarten to high school. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, India. The medium of instruction at Laxman Public School is English, and two other languages from Hindi, Sanskrit, French. The school is located in Hauz Khas in Delhi. Besides it is the Hauz Khas Metro Station.
